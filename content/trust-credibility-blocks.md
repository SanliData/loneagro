# Lonestar Agro — Trust & Credibility Blocks

**Purpose:** Reusable content blocks for homepage, About, Safety, and key service pages to build institutional trust and support conversion.

---

## Block 1 — Trust bar (below hero)

**Use:** Home, key service pages.

**Copy options:**

- *Safety-first. Compliance-ready. Texas-grown.*
- *Serving Texas since [YEAR]. Fully insured & bonded.*
- *[X]+ projects delivered | Documented safety programs | Enterprise-grade operations*

**Elements:** Short line(s) of copy + optional certification logos (OSHA, ISNetworld, etc.) + optional stats (years, projects, “fully insured & bonded”).

---

## Block 2 — Certifications & compliance

**Use:** Safety & Compliance page, About, footer.

**Headline:** Certified & compliant

**Copy:** We maintain certifications and programs that commercial and municipal partners require: [list e.g. OSHA-aligned programs, ISNetworld, drug-free workplace, insurance and bonding]. Our safety and compliance documentation is available for RFPs and vendor qualification.

**Elements:** Logo strip (placeholders for OSHA, ISNetworld, etc.) + 1–2 sentences. Link to “Safety & Compliance” for full detail.
