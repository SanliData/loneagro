# Lonestar Agro — Reusable Service Page Template Guide

**Template file:** `templates/service-page.html`  
**Use for:** Agricultural Production | Land Management | Commercial Landscape Services | Irrigation & Water Systems | Soil & Crop Optimization | Municipal & Institutional Contracts

---

## Structure (in order)

| # | Section | Purpose |
|---|---------|--------|
| 1 | **Hero** | Service name (H1), one-line value prop, Request Quote + Contact CTAs |
| 2 | **Trust bar** | Serving Texas · Fully insured & bonded · Documented safety & compliance |
| 3 | **Executive summary** | One paragraph: what the service is, who it’s for, scale, outcome. Institutional tone. |
| 4 | **Operational capabilities** | 4+ capability cards (title + short description). What we deliver. |
| 5 | **Process** | Steps 1–5. Discovery → Scope & quote → Mobilization → Execution → Reporting (or service-specific). |
| 6 | **Case example** | One case: sector, title, 2–4 sentences (challenge, solution, outcome), link to Projects. |
| 7 | **Safety & compliance mention** | 1–2 sentences + link to Safety & Compliance. Documented programs, vendor-ready. |
| 8 | **Sustainability integration** | How this service ties to stewardship; 2–3 bullet practices + link to Sustainability. |
| 9 | **CTA block** | “Ready to discuss [service]?” + Request a Quote + Contact |
| 10 | **WhatsApp quick contact** | “Prefer to chat?” + Chat with us (wa.me link with predefined message) |

---

## Placeholders to replace per service

- **Title / meta:** `[Service Name]`, meta description
- **Hero:** `[Service Name]`, `[One-line value prop]`
- **Executive summary:** Single paragraph
- **Operational capabilities:** 4+ items — `[Capability 1]` … with short descriptions
- **Process:** Steps 1–5 — `[Step N title]`, `[Step N description]`
- **Case example:** `[Sector]`, `[Case study title]`, 2–4 sentences, link to /projects
- **Safety mention:** 1–2 sentences (documented safety, compliance, RFP-ready)
- **Sustainability:** 1–2 intro sentences + 3 bullet practices
- **CTA:** “Ready to discuss [service name]?”

## WhatsApp

- **Quick contact block:** Same link on every service page.
- **Predefined message:** `Hello, I would like to discuss a project with Lonestar Agro.`
- **URL:** `https://wa.me/14023639991?text=Hello%2C%20I%20would%20like%20to%20discuss%20a%20project%20with%20Lonestar%20Agro.`

Confident. Institutional. No fluff. No boutique or casual language.
