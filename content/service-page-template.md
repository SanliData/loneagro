## Template

### Hero

- **H1:** [Service Name] (e.g. *Right-of-Way Maintenance*)
- **One-line value prop:** [e.g. *Reliable, compliant right-of-way programs for utilities and transportation across Texas.*]
- **Primary CTA:** Request a Quote  
- **Secondary CTA:** Contact Us

---

### Overview

**Heading:** What We Deliver

- 2–3 paragraphs: scope of the service, typical clients (municipal, energy, agriculture, development), scale (acreage, linear miles, geography).
- Emphasize: expertise, consistency, compliance, and outcomes.

**Example (Right-of-Way Maintenance):**  
*Lonestar Agro designs and executes right-of-way maintenance programs for utilities, pipelines, and transportation corridors across Texas. Our crews are trained to your specifications and our processes are built for documentation and safety. From herbicide application and mowing to vegetation inventory and reporting, we deliver programs that meet contract requirements and protect asset reliability.*

---

### Benefits

**Heading:** Why Choose Lonestar Agro for [Service Name]

- 4–6 short benefit statements (bullets or 1–2 sentence blocks).
- Examples: *Consistent quality • Documented safety & compliance • Scalable crews • Local knowledge, enterprise standards • Clear reporting and project management*

---

### Process / Approach

**Heading:** How We Work

- 3–5 steps (e.g. Discovery → Scope & quote → Mobilization → Execution → Reporting).
- One sentence per step; optional sub-bullets for detail.

### Applications
