## 01 — OSHA compliance

Our programs are aligned with OSHA expectations for general industry and construction. We maintain written safety policies, hazard identification and correction procedures, and recordkeeping. Documentation is available for client and vendor review.

- Written safety and health programs  
- Hazard assessment and corrective action  
- Recordkeeping and reporting where required  

---

## 02 — Environmental responsibility

We operate in accordance with applicable environmental regulations. Chemical use, vegetation management, and site work follow label requirements and best practices. Spill prevention and response procedures are in place. We integrate environmental considerations into project planning and execution.

- Label compliance and application best practices  
- Spill prevention and response  
- Environmental considerations in project scope  

---

## 03 — Trained crews

Field personnel receive job-specific and safety training before assignment. Topics include PPE, equipment operation, chemical handling where applicable, and site-specific hazards. Training is documented. We reinforce safe behavior through supervision and consistent procedures.

- Job-specific and safety orientation  
- PPE, equipment, and chemical handling training  
- Documented training records  

---

## 04 — Risk management protocols

We use pre-job planning, site assessments, and clear protocols to reduce risk. Incidents are reported, investigated, and used to improve procedures. Communication with clients on safety-related matters is standard. Our goal is to identify and control hazards before they result in injury or damage.

- Pre-job planning and site assessment  
- Incident reporting and investigation  
- Ongoing review and improvement of procedures  

---

## 05 — Insurance coverage

Lonestar Agro maintains commercial general liability, auto liability, and workers’ compensation insurance at levels that meet typical municipal and institutional requirements. Certificates of insurance and additional insured status can be provided as required by contract. We are bonded where project specifications require it.

- General liability, auto, workers’ compensation  
- Certificates and additional insured available  
- Bonding available per contract requirements
