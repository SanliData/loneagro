## SERVICES — Cards (6 core + optional 7th)

1. **Agricultural Production**  
   High-efficiency crop systems, greenhouse operations, and precision-based yield optimization programs designed for institutional and commercial scale.

2. **Commercial Land Management**  
   Comprehensive vegetation control, acreage maintenance, and asset-value preservation strategies for commercial property and large landholdings.

3. **Irrigation & Water Systems**  
   Smart irrigation design, water-efficiency retrofits, and precision-controlled delivery systems to maximize sustainability and productivity.

4. **Soil & Crop Optimization**  
   Data-driven soil testing, nutrient management, and regenerative agricultural practices supporting long-term land health.

5. **Municipal & Institutional Services**  
   Public-sector land stewardship, right-of-way maintenance, and contract-ready operational support for government entities.

6. **Infrastructure & Development Support** *(optional 7th service)*  
   Pre-development land preparation, drainage systems, and agricultural infrastructure setup aligned with regulatory standards.
