Executive summary

Lonestar Agro delivers soil and crop optimization (toprak ve mahsul optimizasyonu) through data-driven soil testing, nutrient management, and regenerative practices. We support long-term land health and yield with documented programs and institutional-grade reporting. Built for agricultural, institutional, and stewardship-focused clients.

What we deliver

Operational capabilities

Soil health and crop performance through testing, nutrients, and regenerative practices.

Soil testing & analysis

Sampling, lab analysis, and interpretation for pH, nutrients, and organic matter.

Nutrient management plans

Data-informed fertilization and amendment programs to optimize yield and soil health.

Regenerative practices

Cover crops, reduced tillage, and soil-building practices for long-term productivity.

Documentation & reporting

Test results, application records, and documentation for audits and sustainability reporting.

How we work

Process

Structured, documented workflow from scope to delivery.

1

Baseline assessment

Site visit, soil sampling, and definition of yield and soil-health goals.

2

Analysis & recommendations

Lab results, nutrient and amendment recommendations, and multi-year plan outline.

3

Implementation

Nutrient and amendment applications, cover crop or tillage decisions, with documented procedures.

4

Monitoring & adjustment

Seasonal testing and plan adjustments based on crop and soil response.

5

Reporting & optimization

Soil and yield reports, and recommendations for the next cycle.
