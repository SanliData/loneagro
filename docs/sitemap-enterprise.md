## 3. Page-by-Page: Required Elements

Every page includes these five elements. Content of “Service detail” adapts by page type (see table).

| Element      | Purpose |
|-------------|---------|
| **Hero**    | Page title, value proposition, primary CTA. |
| **Trust block** | Certifications, stats, “Serving Texas,” insured/bonded. |
| **Service detail** | Service-specific or context-specific content (what we do, benefits, process). On non-service pages = “What we offer” or “Our approach” block. |
| **CTA**     | Request Quote / Contact / Get in touch. |
| **Lead form** | Inline form (name, email, phone, message/service interest) or link to Contact/Request Quote. |

## 5. Lead Form Standards (site-wide)

- **Minimum fields (short form):** Name, Email, Phone, Message or Service interest.
- **Quote / RFP form:** Name, Company, Email, Phone, Service(s), Location/Region, Project scope or type, Timeline, Message.
- **Careers:** Name, Email, Phone, Position, Resume upload (or link to ATS).
- **Location:** Include Location/State/Region where relevant.
- **Thank-you:** Redirect or inline “Thank you — we’ll respond within 24 hours” and optional WhatsApp/phone.

---

## 6. Trust Block Standard (reusable)

Use on every page (same or condensed):

- *Serving Texas [since YEAR].* [Future: *Now serving [State 2], [State 3].*]
- *Fully insured & bonded.*
- Certification logos (OSHA, ISNetworld, or equivalent) where applicable.
- One stat line: e.g. *[X]+ projects | [Y] years | Enterprise-grade operations.*

---

## 7. CTA Standard

- **Primary:** Request a Quote (or “Get a Quote”) → /contact with quote intent or dedicated quote form.
- **Secondary:** Contact Us → /contact; WhatsApp (floating + in footer/Contact).
- Every main content page ends with at least one CTA + one lead capture (form or clear form CTA).
