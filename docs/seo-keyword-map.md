## 6. Per-page SEO checklist

- **Title tag:** Primary keyword + brand; 50–60 chars where possible.
- **Meta description:** 150–160 chars; include primary or secondary keyword + CTA or value prop.
- **H1:** One per page; primary keyword where natural.
- **H2/H3:** Secondary and supporting keywords in subheads and first 100 words.
- **URL:** Keyword-rich, short (e.g. /services/irrigation-water-systems).
- **Internal links:** To related service pages, Contact, Request Quote, Industries Served.
- **Image alt:** Descriptive with keyword (e.g. *Commercial land management Texas – Lonestar Agro*).
