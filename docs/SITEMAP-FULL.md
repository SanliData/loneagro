## 3. Required elements on every page

Each page must include these five elements:

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|--------|
| **Hero** | Page title, value proposition, primary CTA. |
| **Trust block** | Certifications, stats, “Serving Texas,” insured/bonded. |
| **Service detail** | Service-specific or context-specific content (what we do, benefits, process). On non-service pages = “What we offer” or “Our approach.” |
| **CTA** | Request Quote / Contact / Get in touch. |
| **Lead form** | Inline form (name, email, phone, message/service interest) or clear link to Contact / Request Quote. |

## 5. Trust block standard (site-wide)

Use on every page (same or condensed):

- *Serving Texas [since YEAR].* *(Future: Now serving [State 2], [State 3].)*
- *Fully insured & bonded.*
- Certification logos (OSHA, ISNetworld, etc.) where applicable.
- One stat line: *[X]+ projects | [Y] years | Enterprise-grade operations.*

---

## 6. Lead form standards

- **Short form:** Name, Email, Phone, Message or Service interest.
- **Quote / RFP:** Name, Company, Email, Phone, Service(s), Location/Region, Project scope/type, Timeline, Message.
- **Careers:** Name, Email, Phone, Position, Resume upload (or ATS link).
- **Thank-you:** “Thank you — we’ll respond within 24 hours” + optional WhatsApp/phone.

---

## 7. CTA standard

- **Primary:** Request a Quote → /contact or dedicated quote form.
- **Secondary:** Contact Us → /contact; WhatsApp (floating + footer/Contact).
- Every main content page ends with at least one CTA and one lead capture (form or clear form CTA).
